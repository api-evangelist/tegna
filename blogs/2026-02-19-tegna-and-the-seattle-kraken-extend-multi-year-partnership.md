SEATTLE – Today, TEGNA Inc. (NYSE: TGNA) and the Seattle Kraken announced a multi-year partnership extension, continuing a groundbreaking regional sports model that provides Kraken games free over-the-air on local TEGNA stations KING 5 and KONG. This extension comes two years after the launch of the Kraken Hockey Network, which has made games widely accessible on TEGNA and partner stations and on Prime Video.
